Project Description

Urban Well-being Concept

Urban well-being integrates physical, social, and environmental factors. Our analysis focuses on how accessibility to transit and land use diversity impacts the quality of life in Bangkok.

Transportation Analysis

Evaluates the reach of BTS and MRT networks. Connectivity nodes are analyzed to identify regions with optimal transit access.

Land Use Analysis

Classification of urban zones (Residential, Commercial, Industrial) to understand the distribution of services and jobs.

Buffer Analysis

Spatial proximity analysis (500m/1km buffers) to transit hubs to determine "walkable" urban zones.

Spatial Workflow

Data Collection → Geometry Processing → Attribute Mapping → Accessibility Scoring → Visualization.
